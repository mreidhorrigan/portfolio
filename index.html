Matt Reid Horrigan

I am a researcher of media & instructor of social sciences (Alexander College) & electroacoustics (Simon Fraser University). My current long projects are a book about voiceshifting, the use of electronic media to create vocal characters; a collaboration about cozy horror in games and other media; & a genealogy of Vancouver cyberpunk.
