Matt Reid Horrigan

I am a researcher o' media and instructor o' social sciences (Alexander College) & electroacoustics (Simon Fraser University). My current major projects are a book about voiceshifting, the use of electronic media to create vocal characters; and a collaboration about cozy horror in games and other media.

Articles & Chapters (https://scholar.google.ca/citations?user=g8USNu8AAAAJ&hl=en): research & interpretation of production & popular culture.

Music & Sound (https://nophenomenon.bandcamp.com): experiments in voiceshifting, meter, & chorality.
